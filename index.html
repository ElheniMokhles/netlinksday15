Her invention "Smart and Safe Headphones" took the 5th place in the Stars Of Science April the 12 with a level of implementation and design that reached 87%.

Her Smart and Safe Headphones project entitled "Amiica" aims to protect pedestrians and drivers from the accidents caused by the use of headphones and music devices.

In 2011 he took the 2nd prize in science and technology among 347 participants in the ATB Challenge with his "Free public lighting system."
He participated to realize several inventions on national and international levels. In 2014, he supervised the inventor Walaa Aniba whose invention took the 5th place among 8000 inventors in sixth edition of Stars Of Science.
Currently wael is working on several projects such as failure analysis system of machines and intelligent control by sound waves.

Vision challenge with the cooperation of Orange Tunisie

Respecting the annual traditions of NetLinksDay, NetLinks once again organizes the " Vision Challenge": A challenge open to everyone where any participant, with all his creativity and his expertise, must find an innovative way to change the today's modern technology. Under the same theme as NetLinksDay'15 "Perspectives," the 24h challenge consists into integrating a new value into ubiquitous modern technological trends of our daily lives.
The challenge begins with the presence of 12 candidates. The first 7 hours of the challenge will be critica. Having outdated and neglected technological gadgets, each candidate with his own perspective, must be inspired to find a project idea that is both innovative and above all useful. So he will prepare a short five-minute presentation that will be exposed subsequently to the Jury. This step will lead to the qualification of 8 candidates to the second session.

The remaining participants still have to dig more on their ideas and prepare the following:
• Presentation .ppt
• Business Model Canvas
• Detailed Description


Once time expires, the jury members will collect the documents provided and will choose the 8 finalists according to the following criteria:

• Progress in the project
• Innovation & Utility
• Managerial Study
• Overall presentation quality: Slides, Language …


The finalists will finish their business plan and will submit the whole project three days after the challenge. Therefore, the jury will go to the deliberation of the winning idea under the following criteria:
• Profitability
• Appropriateness product / market
• Innovation
The winner will be announced at NetLinksDay'15 and the prize will be announced at the challenge.
